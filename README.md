# 💫 About Me:
Certified Azure Developer and Full-Stack Cloud Engineer with a passion for building scalable, resilient cloud-native applications that power mission-critical systems.<br>Currently at The Boeing Company as a Full-Stack Cloud Developer II, I design and modernize microservices-based solutions on Microsoft Azure, blending Java, Angular, Python, and modern DevOps practices. I’ve led the transformation of 9+ cloud applications—including 4 high-impact systems—delivering 30% faster performance for over 100,000 users and generating $1.2M in annual cost savings. By streamlining CI/CD pipelines with GitLab, Docker, and Kubernetes, I increased deployment frequency 4x and pushed automation coverage to 95%.I thrive on solving complex problems at scale: optimizing SQL queries across millions of records, architecting auto-scaling infrastructure with Terraform, and ensuring production reliability through proactive monitoring and troubleshooting. Mentoring 40+ engineers has been one of the most rewarding parts of my journey—I genuinely enjoy sharing knowledge, fostering collaboration, and building strong, inclusive teams in fast-paced Agile environments.<br>Outside of code, I’m exploring FinTech at IIM Bangalore and contributing open-source projects on GitHub. I’m driven by technology that makes a real-world difference—whether it’s safer skies at Boeing or the next-generation cloud solutions I hope to help build with your team.<br>Always open to connecting with fellow engineers, cloud enthusiasts, and anyone pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with technology
